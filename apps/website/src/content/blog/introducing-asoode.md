# Introducing Asoode: All-in-One Project Management

We're excited to introduce Asoode — a comprehensive platform that brings together project management, team collaboration, and real-time communication.

## The Problem

Modern teams use too many tools. A typical team might use Trello for task management, Slack for communication, Google Drive for files, and spreadsheets for time tracking. This tool sprawl creates context switching, information silos, and productivity loss.

## The Solution

Asoode combines everything your team needs in one platform:

- **Project Management**: Kanban boards, task management, milestones, and progress tracking
- **Team Collaboration**: Groups, departments, roles, and permissions
- **Communication**: Built-in messenger with direct messages and channels
- **Time Tracking**: Log time, generate reports, and manage attendance
- **File Management**: Upload, share, and organize files

## Built for Global Teams

Asoode supports multiple languages (English, Persian, Arabic, French) and calendar systems (Gregorian, Persian, Hijri). With full RTL support, teams worldwide can use Asoode in their native language.
