# Kanban Best Practices for Software Teams

Kanban is one of the most effective ways to manage software development workflow. Here are our top best practices for getting the most out of Kanban boards in Asoode.

## 1. Limit Work in Progress (WIP)

The biggest mistake teams make is having too many tasks in progress. Set WIP limits for your "In Progress" column to force focus and reduce context switching.

## 2. Define Your Workflow Clearly

Create columns that match your actual process. A common setup:
- Backlog → Ready → In Progress → Review → Testing → Done

## 3. Keep Cards Small

Large tasks are hard to track and estimate. Break work into tasks that can be completed in 1-2 days.

## 4. Use Labels Consistently

Create a label system and stick to it: bug, feature, improvement, documentation. This makes filtering and reporting much easier.

## 5. Review and Retrospect

Use weekly reviews to discuss board health, identify bottlenecks, and improve your process.
