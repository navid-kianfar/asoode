# Time Tracking Guide

## Overview
Asoode's time tracking feature helps you understand where your team's time goes. Track time on tasks, generate reports, and improve estimation accuracy over time.

## Getting Started
Time tracking is available on Pro and Enterprise plans.

### Logging Time on a Task
1. Open a task
2. Click the "Time" section
3. Click "Start Timer" to begin tracking
4. Click "Stop" when you're done
5. Optionally add a description of what you worked on

### Manual Time Entry
1. Open a task
2. Click "Log Time"
3. Enter the date, duration, and description
4. Click "Save"

## Time Reports
- **Per-member reports**: See how each team member spends their time
- **Per-project reports**: Understand time allocation across projects
- **Per-group reports**: Aggregate time data at the organizational level
- **Date range filtering**: View reports for any time period
- **Export**: Download reports as CSV for payroll or analysis

## Attendance Tracking (Enterprise)
- Clock-in and clock-out functionality
- Work shift management (Fixed, Float, Open)
- Time-off requests and approvals
- Automatic calculation of work hours

## Tips
- Start the timer when you begin working — it's more accurate than manual entry
- Log time daily to avoid forgetting
- Use time descriptions to make reports more useful
- Review time reports weekly to identify patterns
