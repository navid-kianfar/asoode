# Getting Started with Asoode

## Quick Start Guide

### 1. Create Your Account
Visit app.asoode.com and sign up with your email address. You'll receive a verification email to confirm your account.

### 2. Set Up Your Organization
After logging in, click the "+" button in the header to create your first group. Groups represent your organization structure — choose a type that matches (Organization, Company, Department, Team, etc.).

### 3. Create a Project
Within your group, create a project. Choose "Simple" for straightforward projects or "Complex" for projects that need sub-projects and seasons.

### 4. Build Your Board
Create a work package within your project. Select a board template:
- **Blank**: Start with an empty board
- **Kanban**: Pre-configured with To Do, In Progress, Review, Done
- **Weekday**: Columns for each day of the week
- **Team Members**: Columns for each team member

### 5. Add Tasks
Click "Add Task" at the bottom of any list to create your first task. Click on a task to open it and add details, members, labels, due dates, and more.

### 6. Invite Your Team
Go to your group or project settings to invite team members. You can invite by email or search for existing users.

## Key Concepts

### Groups
Groups represent organizational units (companies, departments, teams). They contain members and projects.

### Projects
Projects are the main containers for work. They can have sub-projects and seasons for complex planning.

### Work Packages
Work packages are boards within a project. Each work package has lists, tasks, labels, and members.

### Tasks
Tasks are the individual items of work. They can have sub-tasks, comments, attachments, time tracking, and more.

### Access Levels
- **Owner**: Full control, including deletion
- **Admin**: Manage members and settings
- **Editor**: Create and edit content
- **Hidden Editor**: Edit without appearing in member lists
- **Visitor**: View-only access
